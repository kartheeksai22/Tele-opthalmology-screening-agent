How to Capture a Retinal Scan

1

Attach the Macro Lens

Clip the 20D or smartphone-specific macro lens securely over your phone's primary rear camera module.

2

Position the Patient

Turn on the smartphone flashlight. Hold the camera exactly 2 to 3 centimeters away from the patient's dilated pupil.

3

Capture the Fundus

Click 'Open Camera'. Adjust the distance until the optic disc and macula come into sharp focus, then snap the photo.
